

# Prathap L — Personal Portfolio Website

## Overview
A sleek, dark-themed portfolio website with blue/purple gradient accents, smooth animations, and a professional feel — perfect for internship and job applications.

---

## Pages & Sections (Single-Page Scrolling Layout)

### 1. Hero Section
- Your name **"Prathap L"** with a typing animation cycling through: *"BCA Student"*, *"Aspiring Software Developer"*, *"Quick Learner"*
- Circular profile image placeholder
- Two CTA buttons: **"Download Resume"** and **"Contact Me"** (scrolls to contact section)
- Subtle animated gradient background

### 2. About Me Section
- Brief introduction paragraph about you as a 2nd-year BCA student based in Bangalore
- Education timeline showing PU College → Current BCA (2nd Year)
- Fade-in animation on scroll

### 3. Skills Section
- **Technical Skills**: Java, Python, C++, JavaScript — shown as animated progress bars that fill in when scrolled into view
- **Soft Skills**: Quick Learner, Good Listener, Easily Adaptable, Team Player — displayed as styled badges/cards
- Fade-in and staggered animations

### 4. Projects Section
- 3 placeholder project cards with hover lift/glow effects
- Each card has: project image placeholder, title, short description, and "View Project" button
- Cards animate in on scroll

### 5. Contact Section
- Contact form with Name, Email, and Message fields
- Your contact details displayed: location (Bangalore), phone, and email
- Smooth fade-in animation

### 6. Footer
- Social links: LinkedIn and GitHub (placeholder URLs)
- Copyright text
- Gradient accent line at top of footer

---

## Design & Style
- **Dark theme** with deep navy/charcoal background
- **Blue-to-purple gradient** accents on buttons, progress bars, and highlights
- Clean, professional typography
- Fully responsive for mobile and desktop
- Smooth scroll behavior between sections

## Animations
- Typing effect on hero headline
- Scroll-triggered fade-in animations for each section
- Animated skill progress bars
- Hover scale/glow effects on buttons and project cards
- Subtle gradient background animation in hero
